Layout structure
We prioritize clear communication in our brand design.
Our flexible layout structure ensures content is conveyed precisely, whether filling the entire space or subdividing it. We maintain a cohesive overall impression by avoiding superimposed spaces, images and transparencies.

Clarity is key in conveying our brand message effectively and precisely to our audience.
Implementing the Layout Structure
Layouts may incorporate one or multiple subdivisions, both horizontally and vertically. The divisions are straight, rectangular, and extend to the edge, offering the choice of symmetry or distinct asymmetry. The quantity and proportions of spaces are adaptable, providing flexibility in design.

A space can manifest as a solid block in any of the brand's full-tone colours or as an image extending to the edge. These full-tone spaces are strategically employed to highlight typography, buttons, and icons.

Crafting thoughtfully designed layout structures, abundant in contrast. These structures facilitate the clear communication of content and provide the flexibility to tell stories with clarity.
Prohibitions
Frames are not to be generated within spaces.
Every space must serve a purpose.
Spaces are consistently oriented at perpendicular angles to each other.
