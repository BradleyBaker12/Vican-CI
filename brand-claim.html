brand claim
"Enriching Lives through Paint Mastery "

is the essence of our brand, propelling our future-driven identity.

The Vican name and our empowering claim synergize to create the Brand Claim. While the logo engages seamlessly with content, it subtly reinforces our identity.
Design
The “Enriching Lives through” appears in font weight light / medium, while the “ Paint Mastery ” is either
bold / heavy.

The brand claim always runs over a single line. And the “ Paint Mastery ” can be in the Vican yellow only on dark backgrounds.

Positioning
While you have the flexibility to choose the type size for the brand claim, it's imperative that it remains easily readable and is not placed at the center of the design.

When body copy is present, the brand claim's size may harmonize with it. In regard to its placement relative to the rings or copy, the brand claim ideally aligns vertically, preferably flush left or right.

If seamless integration of the brand claim within the layout isn't feasible, it can also find a home in one of the format's corners.
