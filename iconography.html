Iconography
Our icons serve as clear and straightforward indicators of information and interactions.

Derived from minimalistic line art, their style ensures simplicity and directness in communication. Discover how to utilize and interact with them effectively, and learn about their construction here.

Additionally, you can find and download approved icons in our comprehensive user interface guideline for seamless integration into your projects.

Size
Icons are contained within an underlying invisible square area, setting the size and aiding in placement and alignment.

The primary objective is to attain a seamless, accurate integration of icons within the overall layout for a refined and cohesive visual impact.

Placement and Spacing
Icons are ideally positioned to the left or right of the corresponding text, ensuring centered alignment.

Maintain a minimum spacing of 1/2 of the basic area between icons and text. When placing multiple icons side by side, maintain the same minimum spacing of 1/2 of the basic area.
