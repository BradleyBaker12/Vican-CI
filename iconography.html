Placement and Spacing
Icons are ideally positioned to the left or right of the corresponding text, ensuring centered alignment.

Maintain a minimum spacing of 1/2 of the basic area between icons and text. When placing multiple icons side by side, maintain the same minimum spacing of 1/2 of the basic area.
