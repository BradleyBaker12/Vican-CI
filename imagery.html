imagery
Extraordinary moments that are both inspiring and genuine.

We invite you to join us on this journey of passion and precision, where every image tells a story that embodies our core values and the spirit of Vican. Our visual aesthetic mirrors these values, portraying a minimalistic and clear-cut style that reflects confidence and assurance.
Brand Imagery Guidelines
Our imagery is a powerful tool that visually communicates who we are, what we stand for, and the impact we aim to make. Here's how to select and use imagery that aligns seamlessly with our brand identity.
01 Reflecting Our Essence
Choose imagery that captures the essence of our brand. Whether it's the vibrancy of innovation, the dedication of our team, or the transformative solutions we offer, every image should reflect our core values and messaging.
02 Connecting Emotionally
Our imagery should evoke emotions and connect with our audience on a personal level. Select visuals that inspire, uplift, and create a sense of relatability. The emotional resonance of our imagery strengthens the bond between our brand and our community.
03 Consistency is Key
Maintain a consistent visual style across all imagery. From composition to color palette, ensure that our visuals create a harmonious and recognizable look. Consistency reinforces our brand identity and fosters trust among our audience.
04 Professionalism and Quality
Choose high-quality images that reflect the professionalism and excellence that define us. Crisp and clear visuals convey our attention to detail and dedication to delivering the best in everything we do.
05 Context Matters
Consider where and how the imagery will be used. Tailor visuals to suit different platforms while adhering to our brand's overarching identity. Whether it's our website, social media, presentations, or marketing collateral, each image should harmonize with its context.
06 Striving for Impact
Our imagery isn't just a backdrop; it's a catalyst for impact. Choose visuals that amplify our messages and amplify the stories we want to tell. Every image should contribute to our mission of making a positive difference.
07 Embracing Local Flavour
Whenever possible, opt for images captured within our local community. Showcasing our region's landmarks, culture, and people not only fosters a sense of pride but also reinforces our strong ties to the places we call home.
08 Responsible Licensing
Respect for intellectual property is essential. Ensure that all selected images are properly licensed and attributed. Seek out images from reputable sources or engage local photographers who contribute to our creative ecosystem while maintaining legal compliance.
Image adjustments
Enhance images to accentuate the application of yellow paint, while toning down all other elements in greyscale and darkening to amplify the vibrant impact of the yellow hue.
By adhering to these brand imagery guidelines, we ensure that every image we share becomes a powerful reflection of our values, aspirations, and the impact we're committed to creating in the world. Together, let's visually shape
a brand that resonates, inspires, and makes a lasting impression.
