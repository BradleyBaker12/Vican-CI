Logo
Our favoured method involves employing the Vican logo independently,
detached from the brand claim

This choice grants us versatility in showcasing the Vican icon more prominently, all while upholding a thoughtful, uncluttered, and contemporary visual presentation.
The Vican logo is a proprietary trademark of Vican Corporation. Usage or adoption of the logo is permissible solely by individuals or entities with authorized contractual rights to do so.
Variable logos
Vican's distinct logos includes three different colours, available as a comprehensive file package.

These logos offer flexibility in communication, allowing for variations in colours to enhance the intended design message. By creatively combining these core elements, the logos empower the brand's visual identity.

Importantly, colours are not limited to specific products, categories, or business sectors, ensuring their adaptability across our brand spectrum.

Positioning
The Vican logo can be positioned and sized flexibly within the layout – whether as a central focal point or spanning across the entire application.

It can overlay spaces, images, or other elements. Regardless of its application, the logo must maintain clear and distinct visibility, ensuring important information remains unobscured. The logo should not be covered by any other elements, such as typography, to preserve its visual integrity.

It's important to avoid positioning the Vican logo on top of products or obscuring people's faces.
minimum Size
It's crucial to maintain an optimal appearance of the logo at all sizes regardless of colour variations.

The minimum size for the logo is 50 pixels for digital applications and 10 mm in positive space and 15 mm in negative space for analogue applications.
Spacing
You have the flexibility to choose the spacing between the logo and typography, allowing for an optimal and visually pleasing arrangement of the logo.

While you have the freedom to adjust this spacing, for reference, a minimum spacing of half the logos height above and below the rings can be used. Additionally, on the left and/or right edge of the logo, a minimum spacing of one full logo height is recommended.
Logo size with typography
You have the flexibility to choose the ratio between the logo and typography according to your design preferences.

For an especially harmonious visual effect, the rings work well with typography when the cap height is set at 70% of the outer ring's height. Other ratios (26% and 11.5%) can also be derived from this proportion (refer to the provided illustration). It's important to note that this illustration serves as a reference for guidance purposes.

Positioning
While you have the flexibility to choose the type size for the brand claim, it's imperative that it remains easily readable and is not placed at the center of the design.

When body copy is present, the brand claim's size may harmonize with it. In regard to its placement relative to the rings or copy, the brand claim ideally aligns vertically, preferably flush left or right.

If seamless integration of the brand claim within the layout isn't feasible, it can also find a home in one of the format's corners.
